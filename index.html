About Me

Hi, my name is Ameen Qureshi and I am a junior studying Computer Science and Linguistics at the University of Southern California. I picked my major due to my interest in NLP, Machine Learning, and AI. Combining this with Speech Recognition, I feel that I made the perfect choice. I also really enjoy software engineering because I’ve enjoyed being challenged by my various computer science courses such as Data Structures, Discrete Math, and introduction to C++. On the linguistics side, I have strong interests in Syntax, Semantics, and Psycholinguistics. This is reflected in the research I am doing as it focuses on these linguistics concepts while also taking into account Language Synthesis. I hope in industry, I am able to incorporate my findings and other new discoveries in ways that make life easier and let humans spend more time focusing on their hobbies and passions. Some things I’m passionate about personally are hiking, coffee, and baking. In fact, I have a baking page on Instagram @ameenbakes. Feel free to click on the tabs below to learn more about me!
