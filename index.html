About Me

Hello, I'm Ameen Qureshi, a junior majoring in Computer Science and Linguistics at the University of Southern California with a minor in economics. My academic journey has been driven by a profound interest in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, and AI. The amalgamation of these fields with Speech Recognition has been a perfect fit for me. Alongside this, my enthusiasm for software engineering has been honed through challenging courses like Data Structures, Discrete Math, and Introduction to C++. On the linguistics front, my passions lie in Syntax, Semantics, and Psycholinguistics. This is reflected in my current research, which delves into these linguistic concepts while also exploring Language Synthesis. I envision leveraging my findings and incorporating new discoveries in industry to simplify daily life, allowing individuals more time to indulge in their hobbies and passions. I also really enjoy being able to give back and work on projects that can improve society as a whole and love analyzing data with the intent of social good. This coupled with my entrepreneurial spirit allows me to always think outside and to approach problems with a creative mindset. Outside of school, I personally find joy in activities like hiking, indulging in good coffee, and baking. In fact, my baking adventures are showcased on my Instagram page @ameenbakes.
